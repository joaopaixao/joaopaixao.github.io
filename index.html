Curriculum Vitae

I received a Barchelor of Science degree in Mathematics from Virginia Tech Univeristy in 2007 with emphasis in Combinatorics and Algebra. In 2010, I obtained a Masters of Science degree in Applied Mathematics at PUC-Rio under the supervision of Dr. Helio Lopes and Dr. Thomas Lewiner with a emphasis in vector field topology. I'm currently a PhD candidate at PUC-Rio under Dr. Thomas Lewiner studying Computational Topology with a emphasis in discrete Morse theory.
